About the Study

Research Overview

This quasi-experimental study evaluates the effectiveness of a structured nurse-led postoperative pain management program on reducing pain intensity and improving early recovery outcomes among surgical patients.

Key focus areas include systematic pain assessment, nurse-led non-pharmacological interventions, patient education, and early mobilization to enhance recovery.
